Villefranche Sur Mer

Villefranche sur Mer is a cozy seaside town in France, located between Nice and Monaco. It’s the most charming fishing village in the heart of the Côte d’Azur Villefranche sur Mer and should definitely be on your itinerary when traveling in the south of France. Founded in the 14th century Villefranche belonged to a region known as the County of Nice..
